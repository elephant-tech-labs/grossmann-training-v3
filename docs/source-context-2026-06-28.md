- Grossmann Training stands for effectiveness in customer contact for technical teams.
- A recurring core line is:
  `Technik schafft Leistung. Kommunikation schafft Wirkung.`
- The work is meant to feel practical, non-theoretical, and directly applicable to the next working day.

## Themes & Formats page guidance

The master document describes this page in a deliberately simple way:

- Seminar topics:
  - Servicetechniker im Kundenkontakt
  - Führung im technischen Umfeld
  - Verhandlungsführung & Besprechungsmoderation
  - Mitarbeiterführung
  - Schnittstellenarbeit
- Formats:
  - Seminare
  - Team- & Niederlassungsentwicklung
  - Persönliches Coaching

Important interpretation for UX:

- visitors should not feel they are browsing a generic training catalog
- the page should help them decide which depth and format fits their real situation
- format selection should depend on daily reality, transfer need, and whether an individual, a team, or a larger unit is involved
